Ceiling fans can make a room feel 8 degrees cooler by creating a "wind chill effect". The fan doesn't actually lower the temperature of the room but instead makes it feel cooler. Likewise, by operating a ceiling fan in reverse, the hot air that rises to the ceiling is distributed - making the temperature of the room feel warmer.!

If the light on your ceiling fan won't work, it may just be a loose wire or a bad bulb. Before inspection, shut off the power to the fan. Check power to fan. Check for faulty light bulbs. Check the blue wire from the fan to make sure it is connected to the live wire in the home. Check for loose or disconnected wires in the switch housing.
